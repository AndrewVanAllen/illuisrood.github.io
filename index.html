The Finalchan Quote Project


This is a small archive of all the various entertaining, stupid or memorable things various users on the #finalchan channel have said over the years, based on irc logs from 2015 and onward. This website does not of course encompass all good quotes from everyone due to the fact the logs are only of one user and are likewise only being read by one person. Through this archive, hopefully you can take a trip down memory (or memery?) lane and revisit old friends, laugh at old jokes, and relive past experiences. Finalchan has given me so much over the years in the form of friends and community, and I thought this may be a good way to repay it for that. This website is a work in progress; expect more users and quotes to be added in the coming months. In the meantime, remember, post ti Finalchan.
